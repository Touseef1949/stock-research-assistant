# Earnings Deep Dive

1. Verify the reporting period, release, and source documents.
2. Compare actual revenue, margin, profit, and operating KPIs with prior periods and available expectations.
3. Explain the drivers of the beat or miss rather than listing changes alone.
4. Extract guidance and management commentary with clear attribution.
5. State what changed in the bull case, bear case, valuation, catalysts, and monitoring plan.

## Output

- Results dashboard
- Key operating drivers
- Guidance and management commentary
- Quality of earnings
- Thesis and valuation impact
- Next-quarter watchlist
